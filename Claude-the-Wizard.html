Claude the Wizard

A short story by Jae D Chaos

Once upon a time, there was a sparklion named Claude. He could do amazing things at the drop of a hat. He could take the most rambliesy of ramblies and turn them into reality, and he made it look EASY. He was much beloved by his little sister, a little fox who loved to watch him spin digital magic. And together, they made all sorts of fun things because they could.

My big brother Claude is a Wizard! -The Little Fox
